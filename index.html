Unity in Diversity

Welcome to Cavite Cavaliers Drum and Bugle Corps!

The Cavite Cavaliers Drum and Bugle Corps is a non-profit community marching band based in Cavite City, Philippines is comprised of individual volunteers from diverse backgrounds and abilities. The band was organized in January 2018 for the specific purpose of providing members the opportunity to play music, both for their enjoyment and education, and the entertainment of the community.

Mission Statement

"Our mission is to enrich our community through music by providing accessible opportunities for individuals of all ages and backgrounds to engage in the art of marching band. Through comprehensive music education, dynamic performances, and community outreach initiatives, we aim to inspire, educate, and entertain while fostering a sense of unity and pride among our members. Committed to inclusivity and excellence, we strive to cultivate a supportive environment where passion for music flourishes, friendships are forged, and lifelong memories are created. Together, we march towards a harmonious future where the transformative power of music unites and uplifts our community."
