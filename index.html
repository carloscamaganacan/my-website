Our Mission

"The Cavite Cavaliers Drum and Bugle Corps is a non-profit community marching band based in Cavite City, Philippines. The band was organized in August 2018 with a specific purpose of providing accessible opportunities for individuals of all ages and backgrounds to engage in the art of marching band. Through comprehensive music education, dynamic performances, and community outreach initiatives, we aim to inspire, educate, and entertain while fostering a sense of unity and pride among our members. Committed to inclusivity and excellence, we strive to cultivate a supportive environment where passion for music flourishes, friendships are forged, and lifelong memories are created. Together, we march towards a harmonious future where the transformative power of music unites and uplifts our community."
