Welcome !

"The Cavite Cavaliers Drum and Bugle Corps is a non-profit community marching band based in Cavite City, Philippines. Established in August 2018 by a group of musicians and former marching band members with a specific purpose of providing accessible opportunities for individuals of all ages and backgrounds to engage in the art of marching band."

Our Mission

"Our non-profit community marching band is dedicated to fostering musical excellence, camaraderie, and community pride through the art of marching band performance. Committed to inclusivity and accessibility, we provide opportunities for individuals of all ages and backgrounds to participate in a supportive and enriching musical environment. By promoting teamwork, discipline, and creativity, we strive to inspire our members to achieve personal and collective success both on and off the field. Through engaging performances and community outreach, we aim to enhance the cultural vibrancy and unity of our local community while fostering a lifelong appreciation for music and the performing arts."
