OUR HISTORY

"The inception of Cavite Cavaliers Drum and Bugle Corps dates back to August 2018 when former members and musicians of the Cavite City Troop 29 Drum and Bugle Corps, established in 1970's, came together. Inspired during a casual gathering, Neil Camaganacan, Jeffrey Distura, and Macky Lopez resolved to reignite the legacy of their former marching band. Their aim was not only to reignite the musical tradition but provide opportunities for the youth to engage in the art of marching band while providing scholarship opportunities. A year later, with Neil's initiative and support from their circle, funds were raised to procure musical instruments, uniforms, and supplies. Meanwhile, Jeffrey took charge of recruitment and training, successfully enlisting 30 individuals, both seasoned and new. Upon the arrival of the inaugural batch of Cavaliers uniforms, Jeffrey, embodying the spirit of the band, donned the first uniform and assumed the role of honorary Band Master, proudly leading the ensemble forward."

OUR MISSION

"Our non-profit community marching band is dedicated to fostering musical excellence, camaraderie, and community pride through the art of marching band performance. Committed to inclusivity and accessibility, we provide opportunities for individuals of all ages and backgrounds to participate in a supportive and enriching musical environment. By promoting teamwork, discipline, and creativity, we strive to inspire our members to achieve personal and collective success both on and off the field. Through engaging performances and community outreach, we aim to enhance the cultural vibrancy and unity of our local community while fostering a lifelong appreciation for music and the performing arts."

PAST PERFORMANCES

Musiko 2019

Bacoor City has been declared as Marching Band Capital of the Philippines under Proclamation No. 939 Every end of September, Bacoor holds the biggest and grandest marching band street parade in the Philippines. Cavite Cavaliers Drum & Bugle Corps participated in this year's Musiko Festival 2019, and won the 2nd prize.

Marching Band Competion 2021

Cavite Cavaliers Drum & Bugle Corps won the first place in the 2021 Marching Band Competition held during the launching of Cavite City Fiesta participated by six other Cavite City based DBC bands. As the champion marching band, Cavite Cavaliers Drum & Bugle Corps brought home a trophy and P50,000 in cash prize.

Cavite City Fiesta 2022

Catholic devotees of Cavite City participated in the Annual Fiesta dela Reina celebrating the feast day of their patron saint La Virgen de la Soledad de Porta Vaga. The event showcases a parade of marching bands, followed by band exhibitions performed by a variety of marching bands, including our esteemed Cavite Cavaliers Drum & Bugle Corps.

Panagbenga 2022

Panagbenga is a month-long annual flower occasion in Baguio City. The festival which includes floats covered with flowers, street dancing, presented by dancers clad in flower-inspired costumes, that is inspired by an Ibaloi dance of celebration that came from the Cordilleras. Cavite City Regada 2023

Regada, a festival in Cavite City, features a week-long celebration focused on water sprinkling. It begins in the second week of June and culminates on the feast day of St. John the Baptist, June 24. The festival's highlight is the caracol, where the patron saint is honored with a fluvial parade accompanied by the renowned Cavite Cavaliers DBC marching band.

San Roque, Navotas Town Fiesta

San Roque de Navotas recently held its annual fiesta last January 28, 2024 celebrating the city's patron saint Saint Roque. Cavite Cavaliers Drum & Bugle Corps attended this event to provide musical accompaniment during the Catholic procession of the image of Saint Roque.
